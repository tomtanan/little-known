Little Known is a full-service creative studio founded by commercial photographer and director Brit Phelan, with offices in New York and Paris. With a decade of experience working with brands like Verizon, Abercrombie, Captain Morgan, Apple, and Nike, we provide peace of mind by ensuring a seamless shoot and careful attention throughout the post-production process.

We understand that each project has unique parameters, and we bring the highest level of professionalism and service to every job, regardless of budget. Through our combined disciplines, Little Known offers production, editing, VFX, motion graphics, color grading, sound design, and retouching, providing a creative partnership to guide your project to the finish line.
